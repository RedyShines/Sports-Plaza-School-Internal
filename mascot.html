Our Mascot History

Rouge and Rug

Rouge was designed in 1935 by Johnathan Canburry to promote the rugby grounds on the sporting agency. He is beaten up from countless games of rugby. And you got his companion Rug who is a living Rugby ball. He was created in 1955 with the rest of the ballers group to become Rouge’s right hand man in training.

Jess and Base

Jess was designed in 1935 by Johnathan Canburry to encourage girls to play what were portrayed as a mainly male sport. She is really good at breakdancing for whatever reason and the sister of Jens. Base is Jess’s partner in crime. He was created in 1955 with the rest of the ballers group to smack his fellow baseballs out of the park.

Jens and Tenni

Jens was designed in 1935 by Johnathan Canburry to encourage boys to play what were portrayed as a mainly female sport. He is a very silly fellow and wants to become a stand up comedian while running in the court. His sister Jess finds Jens has a very overactive imagination. Tenni is Jen’s number 1 supporter! She believes that Jens can achieve his dreams in due time. She was created in 1955 with the rest of the ballers group.

Cludz and Baske

Cludz was designed in 1949 by Jeffrey Canburry for the new fitness duo to encourage overweight people to get up and play sports. Cludz is very clumsy, hence his silly name. He loves to hang out with his best friend Jocks. Baske is a goofy trainer who believes that Cludz can get in shape no matter what. He was created in 1955 with the rest of the ballers group.

Jocks and Volley

Jocks was designed in 1949 by Jeffrey Canburry for the new fitness duo to encourage fit people to get out of their comfort zone and play a new sport. Jocks are your average positive chad, encouraging everyone to try something new like he has done for Cludz. Volley is a very dizzy fellow as he gets thrown around by Jock. Although he is very nauseous, he is very proud of what Jock has accomplished. He was created in 1955 with the rest of the ballers group.

Marc

Marc was designed in 1963 by Jeffrey Canburry for the new misfit trio. They were created to promote the new parts of the sports plaza, this being the new bowling alley. Marc is your average bowling alley worker, who wants to grow up to be an actor.

Akira

Akira was designed in 1963 by Jeffrey Canburry for the new misfit trio. They were created to promote the new parts of the sports plaza, this being the new swimming pools. Akira is a strong swimmer from Japan. She wants to become a world renown swimmer and win many medals. Only problem is that she doesn’t have strong enough legs to kick through the water.

Connor

Connor was designed in 1963 by Jeffrey Canburry for the new misfit trio. They were created to promote the new parts of the sports plaza, this being the new arcade and party area. Connor is a great gamer and a nerd about everything arcade. You will never be able to beat a highscore on an arcade she has won in. She has gotten so good at arcade, that she is now one with the coding.
